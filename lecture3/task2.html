It is winter. December, January and February are winter months. The weather is cold in winter. It usually snows. The streets and the fields are white. The days are short and the nights are long.

It is spring. March, April and May are spring months. The weather is fine in spring. It is cool or warm. It sometimes rains. In May it is very warm. You can see flowers in the fields.

It is summer. June, July and August are summer months. It is hot and sunny in summer. The weather is nice. The days are long and the nights are short. The fields and trees are green

It is autumn. September, October and November are autumn months. It is cool in autumn. The weather is rainy and windy. The trees are yellow and red. There are many fruits and vegetables in the gardens.
